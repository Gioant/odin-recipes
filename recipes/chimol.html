Chimol - Salvadorian Dish

Description:

Chimol is the "radish" version of Pico de Gallo, A type of salsa commonly used in Mexican cuisine. Traditionally made from chopped tomato, onion, serrano peppers, salt, lime juice and cilantro.

With Chimol, Radish is added to give that peppery, nose-clearing spice while also adding some spice or "chile" to get your tongue tingling. Can be eaten as a salad or more commonly as a side to complement steak with rice!
