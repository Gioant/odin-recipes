Bananas are high in fiber, potassium, and vitamins B6 and C. Peanut butter has protein and healthy fats too

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  • Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey & ice cubes in a blender
  • blend until smooth, about 30sec.
