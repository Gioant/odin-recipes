Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is an avocado based dip,spread or can be even used as a salad first created in Mexico. This dish became a part of international and american cuisine as a dip, condiment and salad ingredient. main ingredients consist of avocados, sea salt, lime or lemon juice, onions & cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 4 Avocados
  • Your choice of salt. 1/2 of Sea salt, Table salt or the best option: kosher salt
  • Your choice of Spice. I suggest 2 tbsp of serrano chili
  • 1/4 cup of finely diced white onion
  • 1 cup diced of Tomatoes (optional)
  • 1 Lime or lemon juice.

Steps

  • Place minced peppers, 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro & diced onions on a cutting board
  • Chop them together into very small pieces
  • Sprinkle with your choice of salt
  • smear mixture into a paste and chop again to achieve fine texture as possible
  • stem, halve & pit the avocados
  • scoop out flesh and place in a bowl.
  • Add 1/4 cup chopped cilantro, onion & serrano mixture, salt and either lime or lemon juice
  • Taste and adjust seasonings
